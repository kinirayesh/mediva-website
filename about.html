About MEDIva

Healthcare with a human heart

MEDIva Healthcare Solutions exists to make world-class care accessible, affordable, and deeply personal — across borders and across generations.

Our Mission

To guide every patient and family to the right care, at the right place, at a fair cost — and to walk beside them through the entire journey, so they never feel alone.

Our Vision

A world where geography and cost are never barriers to great healthcare — where India's medical excellence and timeless wellness traditions are within reach of everyone who needs them.

Who We Are

A team that treats you like family

MEDIva brings together healthcare coordinators, patient advocates, and wellness specialists with deep experience guiding international patients and NRI families through the Indian healthcare system.

We are not a hospital — we are your trusted guide. We coordinate with accredited partner hospitals and licensed doctors so you can focus on what matters: getting better.
